[CloudCannon](https://cloudcannon.com) is a Git-based content management system that provides a visual editor for your content.

## Integrating with Astro

### Prerequisites

To get started, you will need to have the following:

1. **An Astro project** - If you don't have an Astro project yet, our [Installation guide](/en/install/auto/) will get you up and running in no time.

2. **A CloudCannon account** - If you don't have an account yet, [sign up](https://app.cloudcannon.com/register) for a free trial. 

[Connect CloudCannon](https://cloudcannon.com/documentation/articles/connecting-a-github-repository-as-your-source/) to your Astro project's repository (hosted on GitHub, Bitbucket, or GiLab) to get started.
